FAQ
What are AI credits?
Each AI credit unlocks one full match analysis — including tactical breakdown, key player profiles with real Sportmonks stats, value bet assessment, BTTS/O2.5 insight, and H2H context. Credits never expire and roll over month to month.
Where does the data come from?
Real fixture data and player statistics from Sportmonks v3 (official data provider). Real bookmaker odds from The Odds API (Bet365, Pinnacle, William Hill). AI analysis from GitHub Models (GPT-5). All predictions use Monte Carlo simulation (60k runs per match).
Is La Liga the right one?
Yes — we specifically use La Liga Primera División (SM League ID 564), the top Spanish division featuring Real Madrid, Barcelona, Atletico Madrid, and Sevilla. Not La Liga 2 or any lower tier.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes — cancel anytime with one click. You keep access until the end of your billing period. No refunds on partial months.
Do referrals actually work?
Yes. Your personal referral code is on your profile page. Refer 3 friends to Pro and get 1 month free. Each person you refer gets 25% off their first month. Affiliate partners earn 25% first month + 10% recurring commission.
