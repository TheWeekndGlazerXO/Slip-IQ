FAQ
How do credits work?
Credits are consumed when you use AI features. 1 AI Analysis credit = 1 deep match analysis. 1 Parlay credit = 1 auto-built AI parlay. Monthly plan credits reset each billing cycle. Purchased credit packs never expire.
Can I use a referral code?
Yes — enter a referral code when signing up to get 25% off your first month. If you refer 3 friends to Pro, you get 1 month of Pro free.
What payment methods do you accept?
All major credit cards via Stripe. Secure, encrypted payments. Cancel anytime from your profile.
Is the data really live?
Yes. We pull live fixtures, lineups, odds, and player stats from Sportmonks v3 and The Odds API. ELOs update from real Sportmonks team rankings. Odds refresh every 5 minutes.
How is the AI trained?
We use GitHub Models (GPT-4o / GPT-5) combined with our own Monte Carlo simulation engine (40,000+ iterations per match), real xG models, and Sportmonks probability predictions. The AI has context of real player names, stats, ELOs, and bookmaker odds for every analysis.
