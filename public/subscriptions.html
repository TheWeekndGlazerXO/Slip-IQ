FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
What are credits?▼
Credits are used each time you request an AI analysis. Match analysis costs 15 credits, news analysis 5 credits, auto-parlay 10 credits, and full parlay advice 20 credits. Credits reset monthly on your billing date. Platinum plan members get unlimited credits.
What data sources does Slip IQ use?▼
Slip IQ uses Sportmonks v3 (live fixtures, real lineups, xG, odds, standings), The Odds API (real bookmaker odds from 40+ bookmakers), ClubElo (historical ELO ratings), NewsAPI (breaking football news), and GPT-4o for all AI analysis. ELO ratings update every 12 hours.
Can I cancel anytime?▼
Yes. Cancel from your account page or by emailing support. Your plan stays active until the end of the billing period. Annual plans are refunded pro-rata if cancelled within the first 14 days.
How does the referral program work?▼
Get your referral code from your account page. Share it with friends — they get 25% off their first month, you earn 25% commission on their first payment and 10% recurring. Every 3 referrals earns you a free month of your current plan.
Do you guarantee winning bets?▼
No. Slip IQ is a betting intelligence tool — we provide data-driven analysis and AI-powered insights, not guaranteed picks. Gambling involves risk. Always bet responsibly and within your means. Our predictions are based on statistical models and real data, not certainties.
